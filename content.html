Measuring Angles

How does this relate to concepts from the past?

  • The measurement of angles and values of trigonometric ratios were concepts covered in grade 11. If you are in need of review, check out the activity overview section for some refreshing.
  • Just like temperature can be measured in Celsius, Fahrenheit, and even Kelvin, there is more than one scale that can be used to measure the size of an angle.

Up until this point, you have been doing your trigonometry with degrees. While degrees are an adequate system with a total of 360° in a complete circle, radians are also very efficient and have some applications. From this point on, for the rest of the unit and the course, please switch the mode of your scientific or graphing calculator to radians.
