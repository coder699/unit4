Unit 4: Trigonometric Functions

Activity 1: Review of Sine and Cosine Functions and Radian Measure


Overview

In this activity, you will have the opportunity to review important concepts in trigonometry from grade 11. You should navigate through the links below before beginning the unit, and feel free to return to them as you make your way through Unit 4. You will discover that degrees are not the only unit of angle measurement. You will explore radian measurement and its definition, as well as the relationship between degrees and radians and their applications.

Learning Goal

By the end of this activity, you will have an understanding of radians as an alternative set of units.
