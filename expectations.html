Expectations

Overall Expectation:

TFV.01 demonstrate an understanding of the meaning and application of radian measure.

Specific Expectations:

TF1.01 recognize the radian as an alternative unit to the degree for angle measurement, define the radian measure of an angle as the length of the arc that subtends this angle at the centre of a unit circle, and develop and apply the relationship between radian and degree measure;
TF1.02 represent radian measure in terms of π and as a rational number.
